OUR PORTFOLIOS

We seek to attract and retain outstanding management
teams with clear focus on growth and value creation


Our value creation efforts are anchored on five strategic pillars

People

We believe that people are our greatest assets. Thus we invest in attracting,
retaining, developing the human right capital in all our portfolio companies

Capital

We work with our portfolio companies to ensure capital is allocated in the
most efficient manner. We taight returns of 25% for all company level
investments as this enables us archieve our portfolio wide return objectives.

Technology

We believe that technology is the key to longterm, business sustainability.
Therefore we integrate technologies that drive innovation and efficientcies
across our portfolio companies

Impact

We are conscience of the impact our actions have on our world and are
committed to implementing ESG practices across our portfolio. We work hand in hand
with our management teams to align our business practices with
the Sustainable Development Goal

Governance

We believe that good governance is at the core of every sustainable business. AT UAC, we are committed to excersing proper oversight across our
portfolios. This is why we ensure every company we invest in has a proper board structure, with seasoned and experienced professionalals in their relevant fields.
